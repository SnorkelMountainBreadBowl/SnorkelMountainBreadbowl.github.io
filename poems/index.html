Anger

I cry tears that aren’t sad Let them burn and burn The tears are here because I’m mad Cuz I’m not weak No, I’m not weak I shake and scream at the sky Let him burn and burn! The screams can’t reach the sky Cuz I’m not weak No, I’m not weak I shut the whole world out Please go burn and burn The world isn’t in my hands? Maybe I’m weak
