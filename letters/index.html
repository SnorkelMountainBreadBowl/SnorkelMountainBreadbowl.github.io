Letter to Him

Dear you, Do you know what you’ve done to me? My favorite lies came from your lips. My favorite distractions from your words. My favorite songs from your silence. I’ve been thinking a lot about silence, actually. You sure have given me a lot of it, haven’t you? Given me things to think about. I used to constantly say “easier said than done” but silence is easier done than said.
